About Us

Welcome to the Applied Philosophy Association!

Established in Istanbul, the Applied Philosophy Association is dedicated to advancing the study and application of philosophy both nationally and internationally. Our mission is to provide comprehensive philosophical education, foster ethical practices, and engage in meaningful research that impacts both public and private sectors.

Our Core Activities Include:

  • Educational Programs: We organize a wide range of educational activities, including courses, seminars, conferences, and panels, both locally and globally.

  • Research and Publications: We conduct in-depth research and publish a variety of materials such as books, articles, reports, and magazines to share our findings and insights with the broader community.

  • Ethical Consulting: We propose ethical codes and offer consultancy services to various institutions, helping them navigate complex ethical dilemmas.

  • Community Engagement: Our association hosts workshops, interviews, conferences, and panels to engage the community in philosophical discourse and promote intellectual growth.

  • Cultural and Social Events: We organize social gatherings, cultural events, and entertainment activities like dinners, concerts, theatre performances, and exhibitions to foster a vibrant community spirit.

Our Facilities and Services:

  • We provide a well-equipped environment for our members and guests, complete with the necessary technical tools, equipment, and documentation resources.

  • We support our members' needs through various facilities, including social clubs and cultural centers.

  • Our association is also involved in charity collection activities and accepts donations to support our initiatives.

Global Outreach:

  • We actively participate in international collaborations and are members of various global organizations. This allows us to expand our impact and engage in cooperative projects with international counterparts.

  • We have the capability to open branches and representative offices both within Turkey and abroad, enhancing our global presence and outreach.

Commitment to Collaboration:

  • The Applied Philosophy Association believes in the power of collaboration. We work with public institutions, other associations, foundations, and non-governmental organizations to achieve common goals and drive positive change in society.

Join us in our journey to explore the depths of philosophical thought and apply it to real-world challenges. Together, we can make a significant impact on society through the power of philosophy.
